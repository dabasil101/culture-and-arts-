Introduction

Arts Week is an annual celebration at Egerton University hosted by the Department of Applied Community Development Studies (ACDS) under FEDCOS. It promotes creativity, cultural expression, and community engagement.

Inspired by Culture Week, Arts Week blends traditional and contemporary art forms to highlight the role of creativity in social development.

Activities & Highlights

Performances

Songs, dances, poetry, theatre, and live artistic showcases.

Workshops

Hands-on sessions in community arts, creative writing, and visual arts.

Exhibitions

Student and community artwork displays throughout the week.

Competitions

Talent showcases and cultural contests encouraging participation.

Community Engagement

Collaboration with local communities through arts-based initiatives.

Panel Discussions

Conversations on arts, identity, and sustainable development.
