Who We Are

The Department of Applied Community Development Studies is dedicated to fostering academic excellence alongside deep cultural engagement. We create opportunities for students to connect with their roots, celebrate diversity, and become confident leaders who drive positive change in their communities.

Egerton University's Commitment to Culture & Heritage

Egerton University actively promotes student culture through annual Cultural Week, tribal exhibitions, traditional music & dance festivals, poetry nights in mother tongue, and inter-community dialogues. These initiatives strengthen unity, preserve heritage, and build tomorrow’s culturally competent leaders.

Cultural Week

A vibrant celebration of Kenya’s 44+ tribes with food, attire, music, and dance.

Traditional Performances

Regular showcases of ethnic dances, poetry, and storytelling.

Heritage Exhibitions

Student-led displays of artifacts, attire, and cultural history.
